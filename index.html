About

Academic Profile

Professor Aida Kazemi is a preeminent scholar in moral philosophy and applied ethics, specializing in technology ethics, bioethics, and professional ethics. With over two decades of experience in academia, she has shaped ethical discourse across multiple disciplines.

Her philosophical approach combines analytic rigor with practical wisdom, addressing pressing moral questions in our rapidly evolving world. She serves on several national ethics committees and advisory boards.
